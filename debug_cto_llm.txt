CTO (Chief Technology Officer) ki powers aur responsibilities mein shamil hain:

1. **Leadership aur Management**: IT applications, custom developed software, aur web portals ki productivity aur performance ko maximize karna. Workforce ki training, motivation, aur development ka intezam karna.

2. **Software Development**: Conceptual aur detailed design banana, coding karna, aur software development ki documentation tayyar karna.

3. **Database Development**: Software ki zaroorat ke mutabiq database components ka design aur development karna.

4. **Problem Solving**: Business, operational, aur financial teams ko software applications ke masail hal karne mein madad karna.

5. **Testing aur Maintenance**: Developed applications ko UAT/Test environments mein test karna aur production mein deploy karna. Systems ki maintenance karna jab wo operational ho jayein.

6. **Collaboration**: Cross-functional teams (UX, Data, Security) ke sath kaam karna taake integration seamless ho.

7. **DevOps Practices**: CI/CD, automation, aur monitoring jese DevOps practices ko implement karna.

8. **Agile/Scrum Methodologies**: Development methodologies ka adherence ensure karna.

9. **Special Projects**: Organization ki zaroorat ke mutabiq special projects ya assignments par kaam karna.

10. **Reporting**: Chief Technology Officer ko report karna aur unki taraf se diye gaye kisi bhi assignment ko anjam dena.

Yeh sab powers aur responsibilities CTO ko IT aur communication wing mein effective management aur operational excellence ke liye madadgar hoti hain.
