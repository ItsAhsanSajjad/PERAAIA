Answer: **System Support Officer** ka role PERA mein kuch khaas responsibilities aur duties par mabni hota hai. Yeh position **Sub Divisional Enforcement Officer** (SDEO) ko report karti hai aur Enforcement Stations par kaam karti hai. Iska maqsad IT infrastructure aur office administration ko smoothly chalana hai. 

### Responsibilities:
- **Hardware Management**: Desktops, laptops, printers, aur scanners ki troubleshooting aur management.
- **Software Maintenance**: Software applications ko install, configure, aur maintain karna.
- **Network Management**: Network connectivity issues ko monitor aur resolve karna.
- **Vendor Coordination**: External IT support providers ke sath coordination.
- **Record Keeping**: Hardware aur software inventory ka record rakhna.
- **Duty Roster Management**: PERA ke personnel ke liye duty roster tayar karna.
- **Official Correspondence**: Head Office aur doosri departments ko official correspondence manage karna.
- **Financial Record Keeping**: Office expenses aur vendor payments ka record rakhna.
- **Procurement Coordination**: Office supplies ki inventory manage karna aur procurement karna.
- **Periodic Reporting**: IT system status aur administrative activities par reports tayar karna.

Yeh position un logon ke liye hai jinke paas **16 saal ki education** aur **2 saal ka IT experience** ho, jo ke kisi reputable organization mein ho. [1]
